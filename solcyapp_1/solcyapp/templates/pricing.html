Frequently Asked Questions

Is my file uploaded to your servers?

Files are analyzed and immediately deleted. Only the hash and report are stored. We never retain the original file.

What's the difference between free and Pro?

Free gives you 5 scans/month with basic HTML reports. Pro adds unlimited scans, VirusTotal cross-referencing across 70+ engines, MalwareBazaar lookup, PDF reports, and CSV export.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

What file types are supported?

Any file up to 50MB — EXE, DLL, PDF, Office documents, scripts, archives, images, and more.
